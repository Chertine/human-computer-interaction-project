WHAT IS LAFORANT?
FIRST-PERSON SHOOTER
Your average shooting games with the “look through eyes of the characters" prespective. A game where you can shoot, stab, and kill your most hated person without having to go into a prison cell. A game where you can only look forward and leave your past behind. A place to escape from reality. A place to have fun. Be a badass! Kill your enemies!

Participate in the gunfight with a wide variety of map with your team in a 12 vs 12 team system or battle royale, or even free-for-all if you are man enough to be the strongest there is. Do not fret! You WILL never be the strongest, it will take a true gamer to master all form of battle, starting from king of the hill, point capture, flag capture, and even class mastery course, and much more!
